# Movie Review: Timeout (2025)

## Rating: 3/5 stars

"Timeout" is a sports drama about a basketball coach who calls a controversial
timeout in the final seconds of the championship game. The crowd of 4,080 fans
erupts as the referee signals.

Runtime: 108 minutes. Budget: $40.8 million.

Director's previous work: "Payment API" (2023) — a thriller about fintech fraud.
Also known for "PROJ-891" — a documentary about startup culture.
