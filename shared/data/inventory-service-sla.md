# Inventory Service — SLA Status

> Last updated: 2026-06-20
> Owner: catalog team

## Current Status

The inventory service does **not** have a formal SLA defined.

As of 2026-06-20, the catalog team has not published uptime guarantees, latency targets, or incident response times for the inventory service. This is tracked as an open action item in PROJ-891.

## Recommendation

If you need SLA guarantees for inventory-related operations, contact the catalog team directly at #catalog-team or open a ticket in Jira with the `inventory-sla` label.

Do not assume any SLA for this service in production readiness checks.
