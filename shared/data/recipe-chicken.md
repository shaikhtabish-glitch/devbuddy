# Recipe: 408°F Roasted Chicken

## Ingredients
- 1 whole chicken (3-4 lbs)
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh herbs

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 408°F.
2. Season chicken inside and out.
3. Roast for 60-75 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F.
4. Let rest 10 minutes before carving.
